Contact an engineer if your problems still persist after viewing the popular topics. After contacting an engineer, you'll receive an email containing instructions to solve your issue. Usually, we deliver emails a few hours after your request is made, but response times can range from a few minutes to a few days. When you get your email, feel free to reply to it with any other questions you may have. Please do not contact an engineer with feedback about our apps; you can write a review in the App Store instead. All emails not related to an issue that you're encountering with an app will be ignored, because we have other emails to respond to as well. Thank you in advance for following these guidelines, and we hope we can solve your issue as soon as possible.
